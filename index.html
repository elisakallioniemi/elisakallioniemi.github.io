About

I am a neuromodulation scientist interested in understanding the neural mechanisms and effects of non-invasive neuromodulation. In addition, I use neuromodulation combined with neurophysiological methods (concurrent TMS-EEG, concurrent TMS-EMG, EEG) to understand brain function associated with frontal brain regions and primary motor cortex in both healthy humans and in disease models (eg., depression, schizophrenia, pain, stroke). I also constantly develop methods to improve current neuromodulation practices.
